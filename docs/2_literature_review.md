# Literature Review

## Introduction

Artificial Intelligence (AI) has become an important technology in the healthcare sector. AI systems can assist healthcare professionals by analyzing medical data, supporting disease prediction, and improving patient communication. Medical chatbots have also gained popularity as they provide users with quick access to health-related information.

## Medical Chatbots

Medical chatbots are designed to interact with users, collect symptoms, and provide preliminary health guidance. Although they cannot replace doctors, they help users understand their symptoms and encourage them to seek professional medical advice when necessary.

## Machine Learning in Healthcare

Machine Learning is widely used to identify patterns in medical data and predict possible diseases based on patient symptoms. These models improve the speed and consistency of preliminary diagnosis.

## Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)

RAG combines information retrieval with Large Language Models. Instead of relying only on the AI model's knowledge, the system first retrieves relevant medical information from a trusted knowledge base before generating a response. This approach improves accuracy and reduces incorrect or misleading answers.

## Large Language Models (LLMs)

Large Language Models can generate human-like responses and explain medical information in a clear and natural way. In MedBridge AI, the LLM is used to produce user-friendly responses based on the retrieved medical knowledge.

## Conclusion

The combination of Machine Learning, RAG, and Large Language Models provides a reliable approach for building intelligent medical assistants. This combination helps improve response quality while promoting safer AI-generated medical guidance.
